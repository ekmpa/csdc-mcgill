CSDC Student Eliza Yip wins the Student Essay Contest on Youth Civic Engagement by C-Dem and the Centre for the Study of Political Behaviour at the University of Western Ontario!Eliza’s paper, “Engaging or Spectating? Social Media Use, Ideology, and Cross-Partisan Conversations Among Canadian Youth,” considers how social media engagement and one’s ideological extremity influence the frequency of conversations with people who have different political views. Her findings help to enrich our knowledge of the drivers of political discussion among young people.
