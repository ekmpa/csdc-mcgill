Our Research Focus

Learning Democratic Citizenship in an Unequal World

Examining how democratic values, identities, and civic competencies are developed in contexts shaped by social and economic inequality.

The Practice of Democratic Citizenship

Studying how citizens participate in democratic life through formal and informal practices, including voting, engagement, and collective action.

Citizen Representation and Governance

Analyzing how institutions represent citizens and govern democratically, including responsiveness, accountability, and trust in public decision-making.
